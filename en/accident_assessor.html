Simple to use app

By following the guidelines on the app you can collect the details you need quickly.

Accident Notes presents you with a structured form to help you collect accident details, witness/party details, and more. It also provides you with tips on how to take effective photos of the accident and damage.

Capture accurate information in real-time

Our app allows you to collect geo-location data such as date, time, location, weather, and traffic conditions regarding the accident in real-time at the side of the road.

Through photographic evidence you can also retrieve third party vehicle details using automatic number plate recognition and take photos of vehicle damage to support your claim. The app does all the hard work for you!

Complete the report in your own time

If you don’t have connection to the internet when using the app, Accident Notes will store your information and the claim will be sent off when coverage has been restored.

Accident blackspots and fraud detection

Our server collects third party data from the DVLA and MET office to provide supplementary information to the claim provided by the motorist.

If the information provided by third party data providers differs from that of the claim, you can identify areas of fraudulent activity in real-time. By detecting fraud at an early stage you can save money on the payout.
