Job Description

Instantview is looking for an experienced server software developer to bring expertise to a new product idea within the company. This job is ideal for a driven, technical individual with knowledge of system development work, and creativity to implement versatile solutions.

This is a varied and challenging role, to be filled by an individual with both technical and commercial understanding of the software development environment. You will be truly passionate about creating scalable solutions and solving back-end technical and product challenges, making our project ready for web deployment.

You will have the opportunity to have a huge impact as an early member of our team and work with technologies such as PHP, MySQL and MongoDB. You will already understand what ‘the cloud’ really means. Working on a commercial product, you must show an ability to acquire new skills quickly and an understanding of the software development life cycle.

You’ll have an understanding of how well engineered back-end code can improve the experience of consumer apps and you’ll have a passion for building compelling products while maintaining simplicity and usability.

Your Task

You have the ability to use new programming languages and/or frameworks, are familiar with frameworks and design patterns such as MVC. You learn fast, and deliver high-quality projects on time.

  • Backend server communication using JSON and XML
  • Database knowledge for SQL and No SQL platforms
  • Good project management skills
  • The ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines
  • The ability to communicate effectively and work in a small team
  • Familiarity with working on commercial products and flexibility to adapt to changing requirements
  • A creative approach to problem solving

Qualifications/Entry Requirements:

  • Computing Science degree or equivalent experience is essential
  • 3 years experience in commerical software development environmnet.
  • Relevant subjects include Computer Science, IT, Software Development, Software Engineering, Business Information Systems
